About Cathy

Cathy is a dedicated teacher with over four years of experience educating in the tech industry. Cathy recently graduated from Tufts University where she studied Computer Science and Education and worked as a Teaching Assistant in the Computer Science Department. Fascinated by the process by which young people learn coding Cathy went on the work as a Reasearch Assistant in the DevTech lab studying how learning coding influences young childrens cognative developent. In the DevTech lab, Cathy contributed to projects such as Scratch Jr. and KIBO robotics.

After graduating, Cathy went on to work as a curriculmn developer at an EdTech company, developing their Computer Science Curriculmn. In March 2020, she returned to the classroom (remotley!) and has been teaching ever since. Cathy strives to provide a student focused experience with a flexible, project based curriculumn that can be tailored to the needs of any student. Through her years of experience, she has found that students learn the most when they love what they're learning, so she incorporates student lead "passion projects in every class."

Feel free to contact Cathy by email with any questions.
