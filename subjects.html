What programming language will your child learn next?

Explore Programming and Game Design with Scratch

Scratch is a visual, block-based programming language that is the perfect tool for introducing children to coding and the design process. Scratch helps young people learn to think creatively and reason systematically. In Scratch classes, students will explore programming fundamentals like sequencing, loops, boolean logic, and variables while creating games and animations. Scratch classes are great for students who are new to programming and for experienced Scratchers who want to take their creations to the next level!

Group classes availible, recommended for students ages 7-9

Private classes availible, recommended for students ages 6 and up

   

Roblox Studio Game Design and Coding with Lua

Does your child love playing games on Roblox? Do they aspire to create a game of their own? Good news: they can use their creativity to build their own Roblox games while learning valuable computer science skills. In this class, students will learn the basics of Roblox Studio to create their very own games and will learn to code with Lua along the way to bring their games to life.

Group classes availible, recommended for students ages 9-12

Private classes availible, recommended for students ages 7 and up

   

Python Coding and Game Design

What do YouTube, Google, Instagram, Spotify, DropBox, and Reddit all have in common? Aside from being tech giants, they all use the Python programming language! Python is a great language for any student interested in computer science to learn and is highly useful whether your goal is to enhance abstract thinking skills through learning a programming language or to build a large-scale project. In this class, students will learn the basics of programming with Python and while recreating classic games.

Group classes availible, recommended for students ages 13-17

Private classes availible, recommended for students ages 10 and up
